### 1. Classic Beef Lasagna
**Description:** A comforting Italian masterpiece featuring layers of tender pasta, a rich and savory Bolognese sauce, and a creamy béchamel, all topped with melted mozzarella and parmesan cheese.

**Ingredients:**
* 12 lasagna noodles (no-boil or pre-boiled)
* 1 lb ground beef
* 1 onion, diced
* 3 cloves garlic, minced
* 24 oz marinara sauce
* 15 oz ricotta cheese
* 2 cups mozzarella, shredded
* 1/2 cup parmesan cheese
* 1 egg
* Salt, pepper, and dried basil to taste

**Cooking Steps:**
1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
2. Brown the ground beef in a skillet with onions and garlic. Drain excess fat, add marinara, and simmer for 10 minutes.
3. In a separate bowl, mix ricotta, egg, and a pinch of salt.
4. Spread a thin layer of sauce in a 9x13 baking dish.
5. Layer noodles, ricotta mixture, meat sauce, and mozzarella. Repeat until ingredients are used, ending with a heavy sprinkle of mozzarella and parmesan.
6. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake for another 15 minutes until bubbly and golden.

### 2. Homemade Ciabatta
**Description:** A rustic Italian white bread characterized by its signature chewy texture, large air holes, and a crisp, flour-dusted crust. Perfect for sandwiches or dipping in olive oil.

**Ingredients:**
* 4 cups bread flour
* 1 ½ cups warm water
* 2 tsp instant yeast
* 1 ½ tsp salt
* 1 tbsp olive oil

**Cooking Steps:**
1. In a large bowl, whisk flour, yeast, and salt. Add water and olive oil, stirring until a very shaggy, sticky dough forms.
2. Cover and let rise in a warm spot for 3 hours (the dough should be bubbly).
3. Generously flour a workspace. Turn the dough out and fold it over itself a few times. Do not knead excessively.
4. Divide into two rectangles and let rest for 45 minutes on parchment paper.
5. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C).
6. Bake for 20–25 minutes until the crust is deep golden brown and the loaf sounds hollow when tapped.

### 3. Creamy Leche Flan
**Description:** A velvety smooth Filipino-style custard dessert with a rich golden caramel topping. It is delicate, decadent, and melts in your mouth.

**Ingredients:**
* 10 egg yolks
* 1 can (14 oz) sweetened condensed milk
* 1 cup evaporated milk
* 1 tsp vanilla extract
* 1 cup white sugar (for caramel)

**Cooking Steps:**
1. Prepare the caramel by melting sugar in a saucepan over low heat until golden brown. Pour immediately into llaneras (molds) and coat the bottom.
2. In a large bowl, gently whisk egg yolks, condensed milk, evaporated milk, and vanilla. Strain the mixture twice using a fine-mesh sieve to ensure smoothness.
3. Pour the mixture into the prepared molds. Cover tightly with aluminum foil.
4. Steam over low heat for 30–45 minutes or bake in a water bath at 325°F (160°C) for about 1 hour.
5. Let cool completely before refrigerating. Run a knife around the edges to invert onto a serving plate.
